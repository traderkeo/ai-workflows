# Blockers & Impediments

> **Purpose**: Track issues preventing progress on tasks.
> **Update When**: New blockers discovered, blockers resolved, or status changes.
> **Agent Instructions**:
> - Log blockers immediately when discovered
> - Include context about impact and urgency
> - Update regularly with resolution attempts
> - Move to resolved section when unblocked
> - Escalate critical blockers that remain unsolved
